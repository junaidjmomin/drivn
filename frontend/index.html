The Process

How It Works

01

Data Capture

Wearable collects HRV, SpO₂, and movement data continuously throughout your day.

02

Edge Processing

On-device AI preprocesses data in real time — no cloud delay, full privacy.

03

Wellness Score

A real-time score from 0–100, combining biometric, behavioral, and environmental data.

04

Stress & Trend Analysis

AI detects stress patterns across days and weeks — learns what triggers your body.

05

Context-Aware Optimization

Suggests healthier routes and schedule adjustments based on your body + environment.

06

Actionable Insights

Smart nudges delivered to your phone — actionable, timely, personal.
